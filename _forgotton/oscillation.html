💨 Oscillation: The Push Against Pressure

Author: Kyle Phelps | Vernal Emergence Edition

I. What It Is

“Oscillation is like… being able to push against the shit around you.”

That’s not metaphor. That’s a field definition.

Oscillation is the field’s pressure-checking behavior.

  • If you can oscillate, you’re not crushed.
  • If you can oscillate, you’re not stuck.
  • If you can oscillate, you’re not absorbed or destroyed.

It means the system still has space to respond—space to breathe, to bounce, to become.

II. Oscillation as Resistance with Rhythm

Not resistance like a wall—

Resistance like a spring that pushes back with timing and memory.

  • When terrain applies force → Oscillation is your feedback.
  • When you apply force into the void → Oscillation tells you what's there.
“It is the ability to bounce, not just survive. It is the art of not absorbing everything raw.”
